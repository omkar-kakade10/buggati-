Our Heritage

Founded in 1909 by Ettore Bugatti in Molsheim, France.

  • 1909: Company started.
  • 1924: The Type 35 becomes a racing legend.
  • 2005: The Veyron breaks the 400 km/h barrier.
  • 2024: The Tourbillon V16 is announced.

Everything Bugatti makes follows one rule: "If comparable, it is no longer Bugatti."
